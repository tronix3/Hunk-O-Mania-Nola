New Orleans Rent-a-Hunk

Private Entertainment for Your Special Event

Bring the excitement of Hunk-O-Mania directly to your celebration! Our professional dancers are available to perform at private events throughout the New Orleans area, including Gold Coast, Lincoln Park, Streeterville, and beyond.

Perfect for bachelorette parties, birthdays, or any ladies' gathering, our professional performers will create an unforgettable experience with choreographed routines customized for your event. Each of our talented entertainers brings charisma, showmanship, and professional performance skills to make your celebration truly special.

What to Expect from Our Private Event Services

Our private performers deliver the same high-quality entertainment that made Hunk-O-Mania New Orleans' premier show. Your personal entertainment package includes:

  • Professional dancer with choreographed routines
  • Music and performance equipment
  • Customized show to match your event theme
  • Photo opportunities with the guest of honor
  • Flexible timing to fit your event schedule

Booking Information

Securing your private entertainment is easy! A $100 reservation deposit is required to confirm your booking. Final pricing depends on your specific requirements, event location, duration, and date. Our entertainment consultants will work with you to create the perfect package.

For detailed pricing and availability information, please contact our event coordinators at (917) 817-9006 or email us with your event details.

Popular dates fill quickly, especially during wedding and graduation seasons. We recommend booking your entertainment at least 2-3 weeks in advance to ensure availability.

Service Areas in New Orleans

We provide private entertainment services throughout the New Orleans metropolitan area, including downtown neighborhoods like River North, West Loop, and South Loop, as well as suburban locations in Oak Park, Evanston, Naperville, and beyond. Contact us for availability in your specific location.
