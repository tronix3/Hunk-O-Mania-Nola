New Orleans' Premier Male Revue Experience

Welcome to Hunk-O-Mania New Orleans—the ultimate entertainment destination for ladies looking for an unforgettable night out! Our high-energy performance showcases professionally choreographed routines by the most talented male dancers in the Midwest, delivering a show that rivals the best entertainment venues in the country.

A World-Class Entertainment Experience in River North

Located in the heart of New Orleans' vibrant River North district at 224 West Ontario Street, our show combines athletic dance performances, impressive choreography, and interactive entertainment designed specifically for women seeking a fun, upscale night out. Every Thursday, Friday, Saturday & Sunday night from 8:00PM to 10:00PM, our performers create an atmosphere of excitement and celebration that makes Hunk-O-Mania New Orleans' most talked-about ladies' entertainment venue.

Show Highlights

  • Two hours of non-stop entertainment
  • Professionally choreographed themed performances
  • Interactive segments that bring the audience into the show
  • State-of-the-art sound and lighting
  • Post-show dance party included with your ticket

Perfect for Celebrations

Hunk-O-Mania is New Orleans' premier destination for special celebrations. Our show creates the perfect atmosphere for bachelorette parties, birthday celebrations, girls' nights out, and any occasion worth celebrating in style. We offer special recognition for bachelorette and birthday guests, ensuring the honoree feels like a VIP throughout the night.

Our commitment to quality entertainment has made us the go-to destination for women from New Orleans' most prestigious neighborhoods—including Gold Coast, Lincoln Park, Lakeview, and Streeterville—as well as visitors from across the Midwest looking for a memorable New Orleans experience.

Exclusive Group Packages

Elevate your celebration with our premium group packages. Enjoy reserved seating, VIP entrance, and special recognition during the show. Bachelorette and birthday honorees receive free admission with the purchase of 5 paid tickets (limit one per group). Contact us for customized packages perfect for groups from New Orleans' North Shore to the South Loop.

Plan Your Visit

Our River North location offers easy access from all of New Orleans' premier neighborhoods and suburban areas. With nearby parking and proximity to New Orleans' finest restaurants and nightlife, your evening can begin with dinner before the show and continue with dancing afterward. Guests must be 21+ to enter, and the dance floor remains open after the show at no additional cost.

Join us for New Orleans' most exciting ladies' night experience and discover why Hunk-O-Mania has become the Midwest's most celebrated entertainment destination for women!
